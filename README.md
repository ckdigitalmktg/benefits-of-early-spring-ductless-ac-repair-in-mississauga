## Common Ductless AC Issues Found During Spring Inspections

A professional inspection of ductless ACs often reveals problems that developed during the off-season:

## Dirty or Clogged Filters

These filthy filters restrict airflow and reduce system efficiency.

## Low Refrigerant Levels

These affect cooling performance and may indicate leaks.

## Weak Airflow

Such an airflow is caused by blockages or internal component issues.

## Electrical or Sensor Issues

These can lead to inconsistent operation or system failure.

## Drain Line Blockages

Drain problems, in general, may cause water leaks or moisture buildup.
